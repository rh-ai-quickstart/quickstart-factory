# AI Use Cases by Industry and Technology Trends

## AI Use Cases by Industry

### Financial Services

- Fraud detection and prevention
- Loan origination and credit scoring
- Transaction monitoring and anomaly detection
- Regulatory compliance (e.g. KYC, AML)
- Customer service chatbots

### Healthcare

- Clinical documentation and summarization
- Patient intake and triage
- Medical imaging analysis
- Drug discovery and research
- Operational efficiency (scheduling, billing)

### Retail

- Product recommendation systems
- Inventory and demand forecasting
- Customer service and personalization
- Visual search and part identification
- Supply chain optimization

### IT Ops

- Log analysis and observability summarization
- Incident response and automation
- Self-service IT agents
- Ansible and infrastructure automation
- MLOps and model serving

### Manufacturing

- Predictive maintenance
- Quality control (vision)
- Supply chain and procurement
- Production planning
- Defect detection

### Public Sector

- Document processing and compliance
- Citizen services and chatbots
- Resource allocation
- Fraud and waste detection
- Research and policy analysis

### Telecom

- Network operations and optimization
- Customer support automation
- Churn prediction
- Fraud detection
- Service quality analytics

### Energy

- Grid optimization and forecasting
- Predictive maintenance (assets)
- Demand response
- Renewable integration
- Emissions and sustainability

## Technology Adoption Trends 2025–2026

- **Llama Stack:** RAG, agents, tool use, MCP integration
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol):** Standardized tool and context exchange
- **LangGraph / ReAct:** Multi-step agents, planning, orchestration
- **Vision models:** Document understanding, image analysis, multimodal
- **vLLM:** Model serving, CPU inference, cost optimization
- **OpenTelemetry:** Logs, metrics, traces for AI observability
- **Evals:** Manual and CI-driven evaluation pipelines
- **AgentOps:** Agent observability, LangFuse, MLflow, A2A
