# Red Hat AI Quickstarts Messaging Guidelines

## Core Message

Red Hat AI Quickstarts are production-ready reference implementations that help enterprises adopt Red Hat OpenShift AI and related platforms. They solve real business problems with clear, documented patterns.

## Key Talking Points

- **Production-ready:** Tested, reviewed, enterprise-oriented
- **Red Hat ecosystem:** OpenShift AI, RHOAI, Llama Stack
- **End-to-end:** From idea to deployment
- **Open source:** GitHub, community contributions welcome

## Differentiation: Quickstarts vs Examples

| Aspect | AI Quickstarts | AI Examples |
|--------|----------------|-------------|
| **Goal** | Production adoption | Learning, experimentation |
| **Audience** | Enterprise architects, platform teams | Developers exploring AI |
| **Maturity** | Catalog-ready, documented | Demos, proofs of concept |
| **Scope** | Full lifecycle, governance | Single use case |

## Tone and Style

- **Professional, concise**
- **Action-oriented** (Get Started, Try, Deploy)
- **Specific** (names, versions, links)
- **No hype** — focus on capabilities, not superlatives
